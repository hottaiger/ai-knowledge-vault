# Reports Folder

This folder stores reusable outputs generated from queries and maintenance checks.

Reports are not source material. They are derived artifacts that help humans and AI revisit previous searches and reviews.

## What goes here

- topic lookup reports created by `find`
- knowledge health reports created by `health`
- future weekly digests or maintenance summaries

## Typical files

- `2026-04-17-Claude-Code.md`
- `2026-04-17-knowledge-health.md`

## Why keep reports

- they preserve useful query results
- they reduce repeated search work
- they create a reusable layer between concepts and one-off conversations
